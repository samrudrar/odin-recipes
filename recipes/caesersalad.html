Caesar Salad

(Classic Style)



Home

Ingredients

Caesar Dressing:

  • 1 egg yolk
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 teaspoons Dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice (fresh)
  • ½ cup olive oil
  • ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • Salt to taste
  • Freshly ground black pepper to taste

Salad:

  • 1 large romaine lettuce, washed and chopped
  • 1 cup croutons
  • ¼ cup shaved or grated Parmesan cheese

Optional Toppings:

  • Grilled chicken breast, sliced
  • Grilled shrimp
  • Crispy bacon bits

Directions:

  1. Gather all ingredients.

  2. In a bowl, whisk together egg yolk, garlic, Dijon mustard, lemon juice, and Worcestershire sauce until well combined.

  3. Slowly drizzle in olive oil while whisking continuously to emulsify the dressing.

  4. Stir in grated Parmesan cheese, then season with salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste.

  5. Place chopped romaine lettuce in a large salad bowl.

  6. Add croutons and pour the dressing over the lettuce.

  7. Toss gently until all leaves are evenly coated.

  8. Top with shaved Parmesan cheese and optional toppings like grilled chicken or shrimp.

  9. Serve immediately while fresh and crisp.

  10. Enjoy!
